Frequently Asked Questions

What is Unspoken Journey?
Unspoken Journey is a platform where people can share their personal stories, experiences, and journeys. It's a space for authentic storytelling and connecting with others through shared experiences.
How do I share my story?
To share your story, simply click on "Share Your Story" in the navigation menu. You'll be prompted to create an account if you haven't already, and then you can write and submit your story. Make sure to follow our content guidelines when sharing your story.
Is my story private?
You have control over your story's privacy settings. You can choose to make your story public, visible only to registered users, or keep it private. You can change these settings at any time.
Can I edit or delete my story after posting?
Yes, you can edit or delete your story at any time. Simply go to "My Work" section, find your story, and use the edit or delete options available there.
How do I report inappropriate content?
If you find any content that violates our community guidelines, you can report it by clicking the report button on the story. Our moderation team will review the report and take appropriate action.
Can I use hashtags in my story?
Yes, you can use hashtags to categorize your story and make it more discoverable. Use relevant hashtags that describe your story's theme or topic.
How do I contact support?
You can contact our support team through the Contact page or email us directly at support@unspokenjourneys.com. We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
Is there a word limit for stories?
Yes, stories should be between 100 and 5000 words. This helps maintain quality and readability while giving you enough space to tell your story effectively.
